Tax Info Super Simple

Taiwan has a tiered tax system, so you pay more taxes as you earn more money. Taxes are pretty low in Taiwan but they get pretty high when you start earning the big bucks. This calculator will give you an idea of what you can expect to earn at any income level.

Just pick a number, press calculate, and see what happens.
